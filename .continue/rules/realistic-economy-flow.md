Every nation system must have a REAL cost in the simulation. Nothing is free. The economic flow is:
1. GDP → Tax Revenue (based on government type rates × collection efficiency)
2. Tax Revenue → Budget (military/econ/social/diplomacy/intel/space)
3. Budget spending actually DEDUCTS from treasury and provides real stat boosts proportional to spending
4. Companies produce GDP based on factories + education + resources + tech
5. Defense companies consume military budget to produce equipment
6. Education costs money (social budget), produces skilled workers
7. Jobs = total employees across all companies / population
8. Environment degrades from industrialization, heals from renewables investment
9. Budget CONSTRAINT matters - overspending causes debt, default risk, crisis
10. All GDP growth equations must be unified - companies drive GDP, not fuzzy multipliers
